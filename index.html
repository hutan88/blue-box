Can you send a text by accident?

It is a subterfuge whereby one sends a text intentionally to a target which is intended to be read as an accident. It is also known as the “accidental-on-purpose text“, “fake text”, or even “fext” (for “fake texting”).

What is a Mistext?

Mis-texts happen to everyone. You wake up in the middle of the night and send a text intended to reach your best friend – and instead, you send it to your boss. In a moment of weakness, you text your crush a much-too-detailed monologue
